What this is

Every week I take one confusing bit of public life and try to make it readable for normal people.

That might be a council report, a public consultation, a government route, a petition, a dataset, a decision nobody seems to own, or a public service that ordinary people struggle to navigate.

The point is not to shout at the system. The point is to read the evidence, find the proper route, ask a reasonable question and show what happens next.

Why follow it?

  • To understand what public bodies are actually saying.
  • To see which democratic routes already exist.
  • To learn how to contact the right people without becoming a nuisance.
  • To separate facts from assumptions, spin and noise.
  • To find small, practical improvements that might help more than one person.

The weekly promise

One issue. One evidence base. One reasonable ask. One public log of what happened.

This is a civic field journal, not a party campaign. If I get something wrong, I will correct it. If a better route already exists, I will point people to it.
