Make public life readable. Then make it answerable. Then make it fair.

A non-party civic project where one ordinary citizen uses public evidence, existing democratic routes, responsible AI and boringly reasonable persistence to pursue one practical common-good improvement at a time.

One issue. One evidence base. One reasonable ask. One public log.

What this is

This project looks at one part of civic life each week: a public document, decision, consultation, dataset, service, democratic route or accountability question.

It asks what is happening, why it matters, what already exists, and what ordinary citizens can reasonably do next.

The weekly promise

  • Pick one issue.
  • Check whether a route or workflow already exists.
  • Gather public evidence.
  • Make one reasonable ask.
  • Log what happened.
  • Correct mistakes where needed.
