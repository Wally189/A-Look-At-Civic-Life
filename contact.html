Corrections, sources and constructive ideas

Contact

This project is open to corrections, useful sources, civic issue suggestions and constructive collaboration.

Public email address

The project email will be added before public launch. Please do not send anything confidential until the contact route and privacy wording are final.

Email button coming before launch

When live, the email button will open a normal message to the project address.

Reasons to get in touch

Send a correction

For factual errors, unclear wording, unfair framing, missing context or a source that has been misread.

Suggest a civic issue

For public documents, consultations, services, datasets, local routes or democratic processes worth testing.

Share a useful source

For official guidance, public reports, committee papers, inspection findings, datasets or serious civil-society evidence.

Constructive collaboration

For citizens, charities, churches, unions, civic groups, public servants, researchers or elected representatives with a practical idea.

How to make a useful correction

Please include the relevant source, page, link, public document or short explanation where possible. Corrections are welcomed, especially where they make the project fairer, clearer or more accurate.

Important note

This is a small civic project, not a legal advice service, advice charity, complaints handler, emergency route or personal casework service. Please do not send private personal information, confidential casework or anything that requires urgent help.

Response expectations

I may not be able to reply to every message. Useful corrections, sources and practical suggestions are still welcome and may help improve future weeks of the project.
