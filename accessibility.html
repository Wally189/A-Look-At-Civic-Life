Accessibility

This small site aims to be readable, simple and usable on mobile and desktop.

Current approach

  • Plain HTML pages where possible.
  • Clear headings and page structure.
  • Readable text sizes and generous spacing.
  • Keyboard-accessible links and navigation.
  • No unnecessary animation at launch.
  • No comments system or complex interactive tools at launch.

Known limitations

This is an early build. Accessibility will be reviewed as pages, images, charts and videos are added.

Report a problem

If something is difficult to read, navigate or understand, please use the contact page to send a correction or accessibility note.
